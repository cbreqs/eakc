The Timeless Tour Hops & Hemp Experience A Taste of Kansas City

An Unforgettable BBQ & Cannabis Pairing Experience

Kansas City is world-renowned for its barbecue, and now you can experience it in an entirely new way. The Taste of Kansas City Tour combines the city's legendary culinary scene with the art of cannabis pairing, creating a gastronomic adventure unlike any other. Just as wine sommeliers pair varietals with food, our expert guides teach you how to match cannabis terpene profiles with the rich, smoky flavors of Kansas City BBQ.

This 4-hour journey takes you to iconic Kansas City BBQ establishments and premier dispensaries, where you'll learn about flavor profiles, terpenes, and the science behind pairing. Whether you're a foodie, a cannabis enthusiast, or simply curious about this innovative culinary experience, the Taste of Kansas City Tour offers an elevated perspective on two of life's greatest pleasures: exceptional food and thoughtful cannabis consumption.

Tour Highlights

  • Iconic Kansas City BBQ tastings
  • Expert cannabis and terpene education
  • Professional transportation throughout
  • Take-home flavor pairing guide
  • 4 hours of culinary exploration
  • Intimate group experience (6-12 guests)
