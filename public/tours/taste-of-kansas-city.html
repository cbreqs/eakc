Where Kansas City BBQ Meets Cannabis Culture

An Unforgettable BBQ & Cannabis Pairing Experience

Kansas City is world-renowned for its barbecue, and now you can experience it in an entirely new way. The Taste of Kansas City Tour combines the city's legendary culinary scene with the art of cannabis pairing, creating a gastronomic adventure unlike any other. Just as wine sommeliers pair varietals with food, our expert guides teach you how to match cannabis terpene profiles with the rich, smoky flavors of Kansas City BBQ.

This 4-hour journey takes you to iconic Kansas City BBQ establishments and premier dispensaries, where you'll learn about flavor profiles, terpenes, and the science behind pairing. Whether you're a foodie, a cannabis enthusiast, or simply curious about this innovative culinary experience, the Taste of Kansas City Tour offers an elevated perspective on two of life's greatest pleasures: exceptional food and thoughtful cannabis consumption.

Your Journey

3:00 PM

Welcome & Introduction

Meet your culinary guide and begin your journey into the world of BBQ and cannabis pairing. Learn about Kansas City's rich barbecue history and the basics of terpene profiles.

3:30 PM

Premier Dispensary Visit (45 minutes)

Tour a top Kansas City dispensary with expert staff. Discover how terpenes like myrcene, caryophyllene, and limonene complement different BBQ flavors. Personal shopping time included.

4:30 PM

Legendary BBQ Experience #1 (60 minutes)

Visit one of Kansas City's most iconic BBQ restaurants (Joe's KC, Q39, or similar). Enjoy a curated tasting of signature dishes while discussing pairing concepts with your guide. Learn how earthy strains complement smoky meats and citrus profiles enhance tangy sauces.

5:45 PM

Second Culinary Stop (60 minutes)

Continue your culinary journey at a second location, which may include another BBQ spot, a fine dining establishment, or a specialty food venue. Expand your pairing knowledge with different flavor profiles.

7:00 PM

Tour Conclusion

Return to the meeting location with your comprehensive flavor pairing guide and a new appreciation for Kansas City's culinary excellence.
