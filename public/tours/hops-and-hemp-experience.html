An Unforgettable Brewery & Cannabis Pairing Experience

Discover the fascinating botanical connection between two of nature's most celebrated plants. The Hops & Hemp Experience takes you on a journey through Kansas City's thriving craft beer scene while exploring the science of cannabis pairing. Both hops and cannabis belong to the same plant family (Cannabaceae) and share similar aromatic compounds called terpenes, making them natural pairing partners.

Over 4.5 hours, you'll visit two of Kansas City's premier craft breweries, including the iconic Boulevard Brewing Company, and learn from expert guides about terpene profiles, flavor pairing, and responsible consumption. This educational experience combines the artistry of craft brewing with the sophistication of cannabis culture, creating an unforgettable journey for beer enthusiasts and cannabis connoisseurs alike.

Your Journey

2:00 PM

Welcome & Check-In

Meet your expert guide and fellow guests at our designated meeting location. Board our comfortable, professionally driven vehicle and begin your journey with an introduction to the botanical connection between hops and cannabis.

2:30 PM

Dispensary Visit & Education (45 minutes)

Tour a premier Kansas City dispensary with a knowledgeable budtender. Learn about terpene profiles, cannabis strains, and how they pair with different beer styles. Guests have the opportunity to make personal purchases (not included in tour price).

3:30 PM

Boulevard Brewing Company (90 minutes)

Experience Missouri's largest craft brewery with a comprehensive tour of the facilities. Enjoy a curated tasting flight of 3-4 beers while learning about the brewing process and discussing pairing concepts. Light snacks included.

5:15 PM

Local Craft Brewery (60 minutes)

Visit a second Kansas City craft brewery (KC Bier Company or BKS Artisan Ales) for an intimate tasting experience. Continue your pairing education in a relaxed atmosphere with time for questions and socializing.

6:30 PM

Tour Conclusion

Return to the original meeting location with your take-home pairing guide and newfound knowledge of craft beer and cannabis pairing.
