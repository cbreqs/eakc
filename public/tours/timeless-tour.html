An Unforgettable Historical & Cannabis Pairing Experience

Step back in time and discover the soul of Kansas City. The Timeless Tour weaves together the city's legendary jazz heritage, pivotal historical moments, and the evolving story of cannabis culture in America. From the vibrant 18th & Vine Historic Jazz District to world-class museums and cultural landmarks, this tour offers a unique perspective on how Kansas City shaped American cultureâ€”and how cannabis has been part of that story all along.

Led by knowledgeable guides passionate about Kansas City history, you'll explore the birthplace of bebop, learn about the Negro Leagues, visit iconic landmarks, and understand the historical and contemporary role of cannabis in American society. This 4-hour experience is perfect for history enthusiasts, jazz lovers, and anyone curious about the cultural intersections that make Kansas City truly timeless.

Your Journey

1:00 PM

Welcome & Historical Introduction

Meet your guide and begin your journey through Kansas City's remarkable history. Learn about the city's role in westward expansion, jazz innovation, and social movements.

1:30 PM

18th & Vine Historic District (90 minutes)

Explore the heart of Kansas City's African American cultural heritage. Visit the American Jazz Museum to learn about legends like Charlie Parker and Count Basie, then tour the Negro Leagues Baseball Museum to discover the history of Black baseball in America.

3:15 PM

Dispensary Visit & Cannabis History (45 minutes)

Visit a local dispensary for education about cannabis history, from its medicinal use in early America to prohibition and modern legalization. Learn about Missouri's cannabis laws and the plant's cultural significance.

4:15 PM

Historic Kansas City Landmarks (60 minutes)

Continue your journey to iconic Kansas City sites, which may include Union Station, the National WWI Museum and Memorial, or the historic Crossroads Arts District. Discuss how cannabis culture has intersected with American history.

5:15 PM

Tour Conclusion

Return to the meeting location with a deeper appreciation for Kansas City's cultural legacy and the evolving story of cannabis in America.
